About

The purpose of this training is to provide a comprehensive understanding of the legacy Unanet v.22.1.6 core functionalities, including managing timesheets, updating project codes, and changing personal account settings. This training is designed for self-paced learning in a corporate environment but can also be used as a supplementary resource in instructor-led settings.

The training content is provided as-is and free to use. The website, course content, videos, resources, and simulation(s) were designed and developed by Dominique Thomas.

Stock music provided by AudioKraken, from Pond5.

The last update for this training was made on 08/26/2024.
